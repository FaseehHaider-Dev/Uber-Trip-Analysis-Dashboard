## 1. RidePulse Analytics: Uber Trip Performance & Demand Insights Dashboard
An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze Uber trip data‚Äîfocusing on bookings, revenue, trip distance, time-based demand patterns, vehicle performance, payment methods, and location insights.

## 2. Purpose
The RidePulse Analytics Dashboard is a comprehensive Power BI report built to monitor and evaluate Uber trip performance across multiple dimensions. It provides actionable insights into booking trends, revenue generation, trip characteristics, peak demand periods, and rider behavior‚Äîenabling stakeholders to make data-driven operational and strategic decisions.

## 5. Features / Highlights

### ‚Ä¢ Business Problem
Ride-hailing platforms generate massive volumes of trip data daily, but without effective visualization, it becomes difficult to answer key business questions such as:
* When does rider demand peak during the day and week?
* Which vehicle types generate the highest bookings and revenue?
* How do trip patterns differ between day and night?
* Which locations contribute most to overall demand?

---

### ‚Ä¢ Goal of the Dashboard
To provide a unified analytics solution that:
* Delivers a high-level overview of Uber trip performance
* Reveals demand patterns by time, location, and vehicle type
* Supports operational planning and driver allocation
* Helps identify revenue-driving segments and usage trends

---

### ‚Ä¢ Walkthrough of Key Visuals

#### **Key KPIs (Top Summary Cards)**
* Total Bookings
* Total Booking Value
* Average Booking Value
* Total Trip Distance
* Average Trip Distance
* Average Trip Time

---

#### **Overview Analysis Page**

**Total Bookings by Day (Area Chart)**  
Shows daily booking trends across the selected period, helping identify spikes, dips, and demand consistency.

**Booking Value by Payment Type (Donut Chart)**  
Breaks down revenue contributions from payment methods such as Uber Pay, Cash, Amazon Pay, and Google Pay.

**Day vs Night Trip Analysis (Donut Chart)**  
Compares booking value between day and night trips to understand rider behavior and operational demand.

**Vehicle Type Analysis (Table & Bar View)**  
Analyzes performance of UberX, UberXL, Uber Green, Uber Comfort, and Uber Black across:
* Total Bookings
* Total Booking Value
* Average Booking Value
* Total Trip Distance

**Location Analysis Panels**
* Most Frequent Pickup Location
* Most Frequent Drop-off Location
* Farthest Trip Recorded

---

#### **Time Analysis Page**

**Total Bookings by Pickup Hour (Line Chart)**  
Displays hourly demand patterns, clearly identifying peak booking hours during the day.

**Bookings by Day of Week (Area Chart)**  
Compares weekday vs weekend demand, highlighting higher activity during weekends.

**Day & Hour Heatmap**  
Visualizes booking density across days and hours, helping identify:
* Rush hours
* Weekend demand surges
* Low-demand periods

---

### ‚Ä¢ Business Impact & Insights
* **Demand Forecasting:** Identifies peak hours and days to optimize driver availability
* **Revenue Optimization:** Highlights high-performing vehicle types and payment methods
* **Operational Planning:** Supports better trip allocation between day and night shifts
* **Location Intelligence:** Reveals high-demand pickup and drop-off zones
* **Strategic Decision-Making:** Transforms raw trip data into actionable insights
